Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PlaySelf web application ("App"), hosted at playself.pp.ua, collects, uses, and stores your information.

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Data

Name, email (as a unique User ID), and profile picture for personalization.

Game Lists & Comments

Game lists ("Favorites", "Backlog", "Completed") and any comments you post about games.

Steam ID (Optional)

Your public SteamID64 for game playtime synchronization.

2. How We Use Information

  • User authentication and saving game progress.
  • Displaying your profile and comments to other registered users.
  • Synchronizing your library across different devices.

We do not share your data with third parties or use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored in a secure cloud database: Google Firebase Firestore. Browser Local Storage is used strictly for caching your settings.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of all your data. To do so, please contact us at the email address provided below.
