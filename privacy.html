PlaySelf Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PlaySelf web application (the "App"), hosted at playself.pp.ua, collects, uses, and stores your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary for the service to function:

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in via Google, we receive your name, email address (used only as a unique User ID), and profile photo.
  • Game Data: Lists of games you add to "Favorites", "Backlog", and "Completed".
  • Steam ID (Optional): If you choose to sync your profile with Steam, we store your public SteamID64.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • User authentication and saving progress.
  • Synchronizing game lists across different devices.
  • Displaying personal statistics.

We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

  • All user data is stored in a secure cloud database: Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Browser Local Storage is used for caching settings.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of all your data. To do so, please contact us or use the sign-out feature.
